Headline

This is simple mockup of populating the right rail with a jsonp calll and simple javascript.

A div block exists in the page of the form <div id="rr_29">nothing here</div> that is replaced with a block of hmtl content served from another jsonp call to another page. The call knows to insert particular content into "right rail id 29" or "rr_29". The service can provide different content to rr_29 as required.

In other words, the page served up is simple, with place holders on the right rail. The right right rail just has a block id in it. Another service determines what is placed in the block id.

An improvement on this service would be for the service called to recognized the URL of the page calling it and determine what content to serve into the page.
